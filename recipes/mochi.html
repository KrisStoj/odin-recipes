Mochi with Red Bean

Ingredient List:

  • 1 cup sweetened red bean paste
  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon green tea powder (matcha)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch

Cooking steps:

  1. Prepare aluminium foil to wrap red bean paste with and place in freezer until solid
  2. Combine glutinous rice flour and green tea powder to thorougly mix
  3. Mix in water then sugar until mixture becomes smooth
  4. Wrap bowl with plastic wrap and microwave for 3.5 minutes
  5. Remove red bean paste once solid and divide equally into 8 balls
  6. Once rice & flour mixture is finished heating, stir and reheat for another 15-30 seconds
  7. Sprinkle a workable surface with cornstarch. Separate roughly 2 tablespoons of the rice & flour mixture into a ball then flatten. Place 1 frozen red bean paste in the center of the dough and pinch/press/roll the dought to fully cover the paste. Add a small amount of cornstarch and place seam-side down onto baking paper.
