Egg Fried Rice with Chicken

Ingredient List:

  • 300g rice (of your choice)
  • ~150g chicken breast/thighs
  • 2 onions
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 Coriander

Cooking Steps:

  1. Cook rice as per instructions on packaging
  2. Cut chicken into smaller pieces after removing any fat
  3. Dice the onions, crush the garlic and lightly beat eggs
  4. Place cut chicken pieces in heated wok with oil and cook until brown
  5. Add the onions and garlic, cook for ~5 mins
  6. Add the egg and stir for ~5 mins
  7. Pour cooked rice and stir on high heat for ~5 mins, mixing soy sauce, vinegar and fish sauce
