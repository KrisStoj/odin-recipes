Spaghetti Pasta with Pesto

Ingredient List:

  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp and 3/4 teaspoon pesto
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • ~500g spaghetti pasta
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground pepper to taste

Cooking Steps:

  1. Pour water in large pot and heat until boiling
  2. Place pasta into large pot and cook until done
  3. Adjacent to cooking pasta, heat oil in frying pan for 5 minutes, followed by pesto, onion, salt and pasta
  4. Strain large pot of pasta and mix pesto mixture with paste
